<p><a href="http://www.djbrace.com/Synesthasia/">Synesthasia</a> is the second album from <a href="http://www.djbrace.com/">DJ Brace</a>’s Electric Nosehair Orchestra Project.</p>

<p>Part of the concept for <a href="http://www.nostomaniarecords.com/">Nostomania</a> was the idea that sound can pull up long neglected memories. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synesthesia">Synesthesia</a> is something like a crossed wire in the brain where one sense connects with another; a color might have a certain smell, or maybe you can taste a sound.</p>
