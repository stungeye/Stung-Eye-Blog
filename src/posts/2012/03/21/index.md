[Things to Worry About](http://www.lettersofnote.com/2012/03/things-to-worry-about.html)

<p><a href="http://www.lettersofnote.com/2012/03/things-to-worry-about.html">A letter</a> from author <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F._Scott_Fitzgerald">F. Scott Fitzgerald</a> to his 11-year-old daughter at camp, in which he threatens to beat her cat (&#8220;Do you react to that?&#8221;) or call her Egg if she ever calls him Pappy again.</p>

<p>More endearing is his list of things to worry about, and those things which she should not worry about.</p>

<p><strong>Things to worry about:</strong></p>

<ul><li>Worry about courage</li>
<li>Worry about Cleanliness</li>
<li>Worry about efficiency</li>
<li>Worry about horsemanship</li>
</ul><p><strong>Things not to worry about:</strong></p>

<ul><li>Don&#8217;t worry about popular opinion</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about dolls</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about the past</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about the future</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about growing up</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about anybody getting ahead of you</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about triumph</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about failure unless it comes through your own fault</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about mosquitoes</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about flies</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about insects in general</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about parents</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about boys</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about disappointments</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about pleasures</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t worry about satisfactions</li>
</ul>
