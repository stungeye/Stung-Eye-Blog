<blockquote>
  <p><a href="http://allangeske.ca">Allan Geske</a> has been a printmaker since the mid-1970s and he employs various techniques in his art such as etching, engraving, relief and mixed media.</p>
  
  <p>His prints are represented in collections throughout the USA, Japan, Korea, Britain &amp; the Netherlands. Allan is best known for his copperplate engravings, intricate, complex &amp; provocative images that at times evoke the landscape, but also stand as breathtaking abstractions. His etched work often incorporates prairie images paired with replications of charts, maps and iconic symbols.</p>
</blockquote>
