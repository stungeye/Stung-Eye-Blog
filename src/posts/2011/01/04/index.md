[What Could Have Entered the Public Domain on January 1, 2011?](http://www.law.duke.edu/cspd/publicdomainday/pre1976)

<p>The 1976 American Copyright Act extended the maximum copyright term to 70 years from the date of the author’s death. Prior to 1976 the maximum copyright term was 56 years (an initial term of 28 years, renewable for another 28 years).</p>
