<p><a href="http://www.copenhagengamecollective.org/johann-sebastian-joust/">Johann Sebastian Joust</a> is a no-graphics, music-based, physical jousting game for two to six players, designed for motion controllers and smart phones.</p>

<p>The goal is to keep your game controller sufficiently still and be the last player remaining. Try to jostle your opponents’ controllers while protecting your own! The tempo of the music controls the &#8220;jostling sensitivity.&#8221;</p>
