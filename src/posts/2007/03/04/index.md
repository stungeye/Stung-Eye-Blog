#Favourite Dance-tas-tic Tunes of 2006

[Look Up! Get Down!](http://www.divshare.com/download/142987-afa) (mp3 mix)

This is part one of three best-of-2006 mixes; an homage to sweaty parties, energizing beats, and the tangled interaction of musician and dancer. Arms flail. Feet shuffle. We resonate.
