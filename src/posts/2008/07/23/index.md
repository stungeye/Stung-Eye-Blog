<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voronoi_diagram">Voronoi diagrams</a> are  a special kind of mathematical decomposition of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metric_space" title="Metric space">metric space</a> used in a wide range of fields including satellite navigation, animal habitat mapping, and urban planning. They have also been used to algorithmically generate <a href="http://www.mcleodbutterflies.com/gallery/">butterfly models</a>.
