<p>A <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Klein_bottle">Klein bottle</a> is a surface which has no edges, no outside or inside. Unlike a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/M%C3%B6bius_strip">Möbius band</a> band, you cannot properly construct a Klein bottle in three-dimensional <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euclidean_space">Euclidian space</a>.</p>
