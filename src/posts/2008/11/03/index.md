[Manitoba History: If Day 1942](http://www.mhs.mb.ca/docs/mb_history/13/ifday.shtml)

<p>On February 19th 1942 a fake Nazi invasion was staged in Winnipeg to rally support for the war effort and to promote the purchase of Victory bonds.</p>

<p>&#8220;<em>In front of the Main library, all books relating to liberty, democracy, freedom [&#8230;] were burned.</em></p>

<p><em>Reichmarks were given out as change, and were to replace the dollar. One group of Nazis burst into the cafeteria at Great-West Life. Employees were kicked out and some jailed, while the Nazis grabbed all the food.</em>&#8221;</p>
