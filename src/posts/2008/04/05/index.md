When I was a young lad my dentist had an office in the w[Wosley](Wolseley, Manitoba) neighbourhood. There was a bookstore a few doors down from his office. In this bookstore I saw a book of pictures, photos of [painted hands](http://www.repubblica.it/2006/08/gallerie/spettacoliecultura/mani/1.html). I'm not sure if we eventually brought this book home, but I have vivid memories of the hand creatures.
