## Canadian Tories Continue to Cut Federal Arts Funding

<p>Contributions cut this week by the conservative government:</p>

<ul>
<li>$300,000 cut from the <a href="http://www.avtrust.ca/">Audio-Visual Preservation Trust of Canada</a>, which archives and makes available for distribution film, television and musical recordings.</li>
<li>$1.5 million cut from the <a href="http://www.cifvf.ca/">Canadian Independent Film and Video Fund</a>, which helps top up the budgets of independent films and triggers private investment.</li>
<li>$2.5 million cut from the <a href="http://www.telefilm.gc.ca/03/311.asp?lang=en&amp;doc_id=39&amp;fond_id=5">National Training Program in the Film and Video Sector</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p>Federal programs canceled this week by the conservative government:</p>

<ul>
<li>The <a href="http://www.promart2000.com/">ProMart program</a>, an artists&#8217; travel support fund. ($4.7 million)</li>
<li>The <a href="http://www.pch.gc.ca/progs/ac-ca/progs/rc-tr/progs/pcrc-trcp/index_e.cfm">Trade Routes program</a> that supports film and music exports. ($9 million)</li>
<li>The Stabilization Project and Capacity Building program, which provides financial and administrative support to art groups. </li>
</ul>
