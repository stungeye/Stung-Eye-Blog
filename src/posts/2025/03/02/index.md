<p>Alright, music lovers, it’s time for the third and final installment of my Manitoba music roundup for 2024. This one's golden.</p><p>Back in December, I listened to over 300 tracks released in 2024 by Manitoba artists and curated three playlists. The first two focused on <a href="https://www.stungeye.com/archive/by_date/2025/02/15">party tracks</a> and <a href="https://www.stungeye.com/archive/by_date/2025/02/22">hip hop bangers</a>.</p><p><strong>With this playlist I’ll slow down the pace to present fifteen standout songs from Manitoban musicians, spanning genres, styles, and moods.</strong> Whether you’re into jazz, folk, indie rock, country, or pop, there’s something here worth exploring.</p><p>Listen here or in the embedded players below:</p><ul><li><a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLHwGpEAs-3LOiGf6XS1VMLT-Ro0deITVp">Toba Gold 2024 Playlist on YouTube</a></li><li><a href="https://open.spotify.com/playlist/4GxEyFbnYTFbAhWPsj81wn?si=0bd4c56846484f75">Toba Gold 2024 Playlist on Spotify</a></li></ul><p>The majority of the artists and bands on this playlist were completely new to me. In fact, only FIINN, Jocelyn Gould, and Begonia were previously on my radar. Many of these tracks gave me full-on chills down my neck when I first heard them. I hope you love them too.</p><p>This wraps up my 2024 Manitoba music deep dive. Keep your ears open, <a href="https://www.manitobamusic.com/livemusic/">watch the Manitoba Music website for upcoming local concerts</a>, and support local musicians!</p>
