<a href="http://stungeye.com/structured/">StructuredThought</a>, has been restructured as a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiki_wiki_web">WikiWikiWeb</a>.

Unlike blogs, which are linear expression mediums (posts are displayed in chronological order, time/date stamped, and sorted by day/month/year), a WikiWikiWeb will allow me to grow the site not only in content, but in complexity.

I'll be exploiting <a href="http://www.tiddlywiki.com/">JeremyRuston's TiddlyWiki</a> to solidify and explore thought, through pattern, language, and connections:
