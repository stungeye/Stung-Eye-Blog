Yes! That's right! It's time for the very first <I>Stungeye short story write off</I>.<BR>
The rules are simple: 
- Let <a href="mailto:stungeye@stungeye.com">me</A> know that you are participating.
- Finish a short story by the end of the year.

How easy is that?!? - - - > Easier than throwing away your misery in a used cardboard box.<BR>
