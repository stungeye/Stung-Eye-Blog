<a href="http://www.thenewdeal.ca" target="_blank">The New Deal</a> was amazing last night. If you ever get a chance to see them live... you must go! Amazing live breakbeats and beatboxing from the drummer. The keyboardist had a mini moog and 3 other keyboards and some effect racks. The bass player was nice and funky but his volume could have been turned up a notch, (and maybe the keyboards down a notch). I would have liked to have heard some vocals in the mix, but I can't really complain.

The Open Mic Jam @ the Collective was canceled.

Looks like Winnipeg is getting some <a href="http://www.winnipegfreepress.com/subscriber/front/story/633701p-771870c.html" target_blank="">bad press</a> over in the UK. (In other news, the free press staff are on strike.)

Check out the newish Flaming Lips album <a href="http://www.flaminglips.com/main.php" target="_blank">online</a>. I really enjoyed track #9, <i>Do you realize</i>.
