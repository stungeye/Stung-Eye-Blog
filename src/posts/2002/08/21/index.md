Two new tracks have been added to the <a href="http://www.stungeye.com/sounds.htm">sounds</a> page. The first one, <i>Relax</i>, was made by myself and my friend Jake. The lyrics to this track can be found on the <a href="http://www.stungeye.com/words.htm">words</a> page under the title: <i>Taking Cover with Wally Glutton</i>.

Any constructive criticism you have concerning the tracks is always welcome.
