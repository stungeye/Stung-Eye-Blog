The first two nights of Peg City Holla have been amazing. Check out <a href="http://www.pegcity.com" target="_blank">the peg city holla site</a> for more info and then come down to <a href="http://www.avenue333.com" target="_blank">the Avenue</a> tonight (August 31st) for some high quality hip hop.

Last night made me really happy. I had a look around while DJ Dopey (Canada DMC champ) was mixing and scratching. Smiles everywhere. You know that scene in <i>The 5th Element</i> where Leeloo is studying human culture and she is fixating on all the bad in the world? Last night was the exact opposite of that.
