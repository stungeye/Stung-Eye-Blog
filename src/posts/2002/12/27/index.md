Christmas was nice. Time well spent with family and friends. On Christmas day we discovered that 25 years ago my aunt lived in the same apartment as us. But get this... not only the same block, but the very same suite! That's Winnipeg for ya.

I spent the day remixing and re-mastering the hypnotic track Jake and I made earlier this year. It is now available for download in the <a href="http://www.stungeye.com/sounds.htm">sounds</a> section under the name <i>Relax (Glutton and Steak Beat Generation Edit)</i>. To those of you who have heard this song before, it isn't all that different... just some tweaking of levels and EQ settings. Let me know what you think.
