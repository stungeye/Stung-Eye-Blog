## You Know Who "Gets it"?

<p>The <a href="http://hypem.com/">Hypemachine</a>, <a href="http://listen.grooveshark.com/">Groveshark</a> and <a href="http://soundcloud.com/">SoundCloud</a> understand that the future will be streamed.</p>

<p>You can listen to (in their entirety) every album of <a href="http://hypem.com/zeitgeist/2009/albums">the Hypem top 50 of 2009</a>. The top 50 was crowd-sourced from the top 10 lists of over 500 bloggers. The albums are hosted by Groveshark.</p>

<p>Yes, the <a href="http://hypem.com/popular">Hypem leaderboard</a> gets gamed now and then, and it sometimes get clogged with meme-ooze, but the web&#8217;s messy like that.</p>

<p>SoundCloud is a <a href="http://soundcloud.com/groups">mix-hunters paradise</a>. I&#8217;m listening to <a href="http://soundcloud.com/dj-czech/bucket-of-grease">Dj Czech&#8217;s &#8220;Bucket Of Grease&#8221;</a> mix right now.</p>
