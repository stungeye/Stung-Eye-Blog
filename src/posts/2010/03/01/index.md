<p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nG_PbHVW5cQ">The future of games</a>, a talk by <a href="http://www.schellgames.com/">Jesse Schell</a>. [28 minutes]</p>

<p>Even if you’re not a gamer this is a must-watch video, especially the second half on the implications of “games that break through the reality barrier” and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attention_economy">the attention economy</a>. I’m not sure I welcome the “gameification” of life, but it does feel like the inevitable progression of the capitalist spectacle. <a href="http://www.kk.org/thetechnium/archives/2010/02/the_game-ified.php">“A world where points are distributed for paying attention — to ads, activities, or other people.”</a></p>
