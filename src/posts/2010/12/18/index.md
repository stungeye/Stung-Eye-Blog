<p>Also: <a href="http://musicmachinery.com/2010/12/17/songcards-an-untapped-app/">SoundCards</a>, music trading-cards that link you to music in the cloud.</p>

<blockquote>
  <ul><li>Many people have a physical connection with their music.  These people like to organize, display and interact with their music via the containers (album covers, cd cases).</li>
  <li>Music is a highly social medium.  People enjoy sharing music with others.  People learn about new music from others in their social circle.</li>
  <li>The location where music is stored will likely switch from devices managed by the listener to devices managed by a music service.  In the future, a music purchaser will purchase the right to listen to a particular song, while the actual music data will remain managed by the music service.</li>
  </ul></blockquote>
