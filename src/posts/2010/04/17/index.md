[Twitter Annotations](http://groups.google.com/group/twitter-api-announce/browse_thread/thread/fa5da2608865453?hl=en&pli=1)

<p>Tweets may soon include optional payloads of structured data. The purpose and usage of these annotations?</p>

<p><em>&#8220;Annotations are a blank slate that lend themselves to myriad divergent use cases. We want to provide open-ended utility for all the developers to innovate on top of.&#8221;</em><br/><em>&#8212; <a href="http://groups.google.com/group/twitter-api-announce/browse_thread/thread/fa5da2608865453?hl=en&amp;pli=1">Marcel Molina - Early look at Annotations</a></em></p>

<p>Possible Useful Annotations:</p>

<ul>
<li>The full URL of all shortened URLs that appear within a tweet.</li>
<li>Longitude/latitude geo-locations when tweeting from GPS-enabled devices.</li>
<li>Re-tweet history and/or attribution information.</li>
<li>Multimedia URLs. Link a tweet to images, videos, and sound files. </li>
<li>Multimedia data. The system should eventually support 2K payloads containing small images, sound clips, MIDI, etc.</li>
<li><a href="http://microformats.org/wiki/Main_Page#Specifications">Microformats</a></li>
</ul>
