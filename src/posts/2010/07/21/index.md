## Request for Feedback: WinnipegElection.ca

<p>Hi folks,</p>

<p>On October 27 of this year the citizens of Winnipeg go to the polls to elect their Mayor, their City Councillors and their School Trustees.</p>

<p>Making an informed vote requires that you know the candidates, their platforms and the issues they discuss.</p>

<p><strong>This is why we’ve created <a href="http://winnipegelection.ca">WinnipegElection.ca</a>,</strong> a citizen driven website for the upcoming Winnipeg general election.</p>

<p>Our public launch is later this month, but we’d love you to <a href="http://winnipegelection.ca">take a sneak peak</a>.</p>

<p>We’ll use your feedback to determine how to proceed with the site.</p>

<p>Currently you’ll find:</p>

<ul>
<li><a href="http://winnipegelection.ca/election_info">General Election Information</a></li>
<li><a href="http://winnipegelection.ca/info/mayor">Mayoral Candidates</a></li>
<li><a href="http://winnipegelection.ca/info/council">City Council Candidates</a></li>
<li><a href="http://winnipegelection.ca">A <em>Colourful</em> Interactive Candidate Map</a></li>
</ul>
