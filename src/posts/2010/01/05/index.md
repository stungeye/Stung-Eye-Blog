["On The Shortness Of Life"](http://www.philosophicalsociety.com/Archives/On%20The%20Shortness%20Of%20Life.htm)

<p><em>&#8220;It is not that we have so little time but that we lose so much. [W]hen it is squandered through luxury and indifference, and spent for no good end, we realize it has gone, under the pressure of the ultimate necessity, before we were aware it was going.</em></p>

<p><em>So it is: the life we receive is not short but we make it so; we are not ill provided but use what we have wastefully.&#8221;</em> &#8212; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seneca_the_Younger">Seneca the Younger</a></p>
