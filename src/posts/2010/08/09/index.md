[Rain Caddy - Should You Water Your Lawn Today?](http://makeitrain.heroku.com/)

<p><a href="http://www.kingbain.com/">John</a>, a previous student of mine, made <a href="http://makeitrain.heroku.com/">this weather-aware application</a> and entered it in the <a href="http://apps4climateaction.gov.bc.ca/">Apps 4 Climate Action contest</a>.</p>

<p>Enter your (Canadian) address to see the average rainfall in your area over the last 7 days. John&#8217;s site uses data from <a href="http://climate.weatheroffice.gc.ca/Welcome_e.html">Environment Canada</a>&#8217;s 1079 weather stations. (See the  <a href="http://makeitrain.heroku.com/main/about">Rain Caddy About page</a> for a map of all these stations.)</p>

<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_science_data">Open Data</a> for the win.</p>
