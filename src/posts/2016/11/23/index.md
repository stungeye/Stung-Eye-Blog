<p><a href="http://opendemocracymanitoba.ca/">Open Democracy Manitoba</a> (ODM) is now a registered non-profit corporation!</p>
<p>I helped found ODM six years ago, in the summer of 2010. Since then we&rsquo;ve helped hundreds of thousands of voters research their candidates and learn about their local democratic process by way of <a href="http://winnipegelection.ca">WinnipegElection.ca</a> and <a href="http://manitobaelection.ca">ManitobaElection.ca.</a></p>
<p>We recently launched <a href="http://winnipegelected.ca">WinnipegElected.ca</a>, a site where citizen can easily follow Winnipeg City Council decisions on reports, motions and by laws. The site was developed with the assistance of the <a href="http://www.winnipeg.ca/clerks/">Winnipeg Clerk&rsquo;s Department</a>.</p>
<p>Incorporating as a non-profit will help us in securing grants, allowing us to continue to empower the citizens of Winnipeg, Manitoba, and beyond, through our online tools and resources.</p>
