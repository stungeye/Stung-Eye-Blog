[Culture Machine - Pirate Philosophy (Vol. 10)](http://culturemachine.net/index.php/cm/issue/current)

<p>Culture Machine explores the culture and philosophy of piracy (as in P2P, remixing, culture-jamming, etc, not peg-legs and parrots).</p>

<p><strong>My Thoughts:</strong></p>

<p>Digital piracy is mainstream. Nearly all of popular culture (music, tv, movies, books, etc) can be acquired and consumed for free. Kevin Kelly tells us that <a href="http://www.kk.org/thetechnium/archives/2009/01/better_than_own.php">ownership is no longer important</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Prediction:</strong></p>

<p>The next step will be the wide-scale piracy of physical objects. More on this at another time&#8230;</p>
