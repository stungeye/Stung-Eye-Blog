[Wu wei](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wu_wei)

<p>Natural action. Creative quietude. The art of letting-be.</p>

<p>Wu wei involves knowing when to act and when not to act.</p>

<p><em>The Sage is occupied with the unspoken
and acts without effort.</em></p>

<p><em>Teaching without verbosity,
producing without possessing,
creating without regard to result,
claiming nothing,
the Sage has nothing to lose.</em> -<a href="http://www.yellowbridge.com/onlinelit/daodejing.php">Tao Te Ching</a></p>
