[Kashiwa Mystery Cafe](http://www.cabel.name/2009/09/kashiwa-mystery-cafe.html)

<p>A cafe in Kashiwa Japan were you order for the next person in line, and receive what was ordered by the person in front of you.</p>

<p>The rules:</p>

<ul>
<li>Let&#8217;s treat the next person. What to treat them with? It&#8217;s your choice.</li>
<li>Even if it&#8217;s a group of friends or a family, please form a single-file line. Also, you can&#8217;t buy twice in a row.</li>
<li>Please enjoy what you get, even if you hate it. (If you really, really hate it, let&#8217;s quietly give it to another while saying, &#8220;It&#8217;s my treat…&#8221;)</li>
<li>Let&#8217;s say &#8220;Thank You! (Gochihosama)&#8221; if you find the person with your Ogori cafe card.</li>
</ul>
