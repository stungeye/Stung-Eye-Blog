[5000 Fans](http://www.scottandrew.com/wordpress/archives/2005/04/5000_fans.html)

<p>An artist who has 5000 hardcore fans to give him or her $20 each year (for their music, art, etc) stands to make $100K per year.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/WeAreTheMusicMakers/comments/81568/an_artist_who_has_5000_hardcore_fans_to_give_him/c07ysmd">or</a>: 100,000 fans @ $1 (digital download) or 1,000,000 fans @ 10 cents (micropayment)!</p>

<p>Sure, there might be overhead costs in the production of said music/art, but even if this overhead was an outrageous %50 of the profit, you&#8217;d still be making $50K per year.</p>
