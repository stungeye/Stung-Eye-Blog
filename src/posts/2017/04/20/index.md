<p>I&rsquo;ll be speaking at Papers We Love again this year <a href="http://pwlwpg.ca/events/2017-05-24-kyle-geske/">on May 24th</a>. This year&rsquo;s paper will be <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1601.06176.pdf">Christoph Adami&rsquo;s What is Information?</a></p>

<p>This presentation will carefully introduce the concepts of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_theory">entropy and information</a>, explaining them intuitively while still rigorously defined.
