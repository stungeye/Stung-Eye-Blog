<UL>
<LI>Con5traint, a five list game.</LI>
<LI>List, a five word <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meme" target="_blank">meme</a>.</LI>
<LI>Word: one or more characters.</LI>
<LI>Character: Letter, Number, or Punctuation.</LI>
<LI>Five Lists, form one thought.</LI></UL>
The instructions are con5trained.<BR>

<I>Once mastered, Words can be constrained to five or less Characters.</I>

<I>Experienced players, may choose to restrict Words to </I>exactly<I> five Characters.</I>

<I>Grammar, Language and Structure can be ignored, or <a href="http://newlibrary.org/nl12.html#Anchor1" target="_blank">remixed</a>, for poetic effect.</I>
