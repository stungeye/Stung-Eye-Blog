I came across a <a href="http://www.theeducatedmachine.com/carole/archives/2004/01/02/monkey_tail_plant.html" target="_blank">monkey tail plant</a> while wandering within the woods. Soon after, I was approached by two men with tape recorders strapped to their backs. <a href="http://www.traffic-island.co.uk/" target="_blank">They wanted the music that was playing in my walkman</a>. I explained my search to them; the quest for <a href="http://www11.ocn.ne.jp/%7Eorigami/index.htm" target="_blank">contemporary origami</a>. Hopefully the origami <a href="http://www.undergroundpress.org/" target="_blank">zine</a> I picked up in town would provide the necessary clues.

I continued <a href="http://www.theeducatedmachine.com/neueshue/archives/001253.html" target="_blank">walking</a>. My mind began to drift back to my warm room and the meditative nature of <a href="http://www.craftster.org/" target="_blank">crafts</a>.

Lost in thought, an idea arose: "<a href="http://dominionpaper.ca/features/2004/02/03/what_if_we.html" target="_blank">What if we gave it all away?</a>"
