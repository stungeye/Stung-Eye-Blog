Recently at the Detroit auto show, Ford was displaying a concept car called the <a href="http://www.evworld.com/databases/storybuilder.cfm?storyid=481">Model U</a>, a great little piece of work.  Here's a quick run down of some of the features:
<UL>
<LI>Hydrogen Engine
<LI>Recycleable polyester seat fabric
<LI>Corn-based tire fibres
<LI>Sunflower seed engine oil
<LI>Soy-based seat foam
<LI>Corn-based retractable (and completely compostable) roof
<LI>Soy-based resin tailgale
<LI>Clear-coat paint that replaces petroleum-based materials with plant-based ones.
</UL>
