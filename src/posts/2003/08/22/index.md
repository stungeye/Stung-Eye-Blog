There are organizations like <a href="http://www.icomm.ca/macos/" target="_blank">MACOS</a> that encourage sample use but for the most part the legality of samples is often quite a grey area. The <a href="http://www.nettime.org/Lists-Archives/nettime-l-0005/msg00146.html" target="_blank">Beastie Boys were sued in 2000</a> for using a 6 second flute sample of James Newton for their track 'Pass the mic'. Turns out they cleared the sample with his record label but failed to ask Mr. Newton for permission. The Beasties won and <a href="http://www.geeklife.com/article.aspx?articleid=208&amp;CommentCount=187" target="_blank">counter sued Mr. Newton</a> in 2002 for nearly $500,000 in court fees.
