Spring has sprung. I feel as if I am lost in a sea of wonder. It's a good kind of lost. It has been a good week.<br>
Take a look around yourself. The world is full of so many interesting and exciting new experiences. Whether it be the intricate lines of a <a href="http://woodblock.com/webcam/main_webcam.html" target=_blank>Japanese woodblock print</a>, the <a href="http://www.soulcatcherstudio.com/exhibitions/blossfeldt/index.htm" target=_blank>mathematical patterns seen in plants</a>, <a href="http://www.hugemagazine.com/thrift/index.html" target=_blank>art found at a local thrift shop</a>,  the <a href="http://www.mrming.co.uk/" target=_blank>headlines as seen through the eyes of a visual artists</a>, the <a href="http://videoart.net/home/artists/ArtistPage.cfm?Artist_ID=90" target=_blank>video art of an ex-student of my father</a>, or <a href="http://www.boobytrap.org/" target=_blank>an action-packed flash movie</a>. <BR>
