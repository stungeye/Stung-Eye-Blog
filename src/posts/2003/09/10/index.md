"First things first," he spit out as he barged through the door unannounced.<BR>
"Excuse me?” I replied rubbing the sleep that had recently accumulated in the corners of my eyes.<BR>
"I'm here to set things straight," he continued unfazed.<BR>
“Set what things straight?”<BR>
“The corners. The angles. The parts of speech that fall on deaf ears!”<BR>
On any other day the sudden arrival of a strange man dressed all in black spouting nonsensical clichés would have sent my mental faculties off to the land of fear and paranoia. This morning however, I felt ready for anything. Although my memories of last night were foggy at best, the drugs must have been of the highest quality.<BR>
“Lets start at the beginning!” He exclaimed, so that is where I too will begin.<BR>
<I>The beginning…</I>
