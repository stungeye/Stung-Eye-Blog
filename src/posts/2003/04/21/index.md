I was listening to an old <a href="http://www.buck65.com" target="_blank">Buck 65</a> album this morning, (Game Tight), and the bass sample used on the first track, (Three Up Three Down), sounded very familiar. Five minutes ago I loaded up the same Buck album and set my winamp playlist to random. I nearly jumped off my chair when after playing <i>Three Up Three Down</i> winamp decided to play me <a href="http://djgreyboy.com/logic/" target="_blank">Greyboy</a>'s track <i>Instantly</i> which uses the very same bass sample! Maybe this is only freaky to me... but things like this have been happening quite a bit lately. Especially with music. (<b>Side note</b>: Buck will be playing here at the <a href="http://www.pyramid7.com" target="_blank">Pyramid</a> on Wednesday night. Dj Co-Op will be opening. Dope.)

Speaking of music, I placed the lyrics to <i>(Guilty) Invocations</i> on the <a href="http://www.stungeye.com/words.htm">words</a> page.

<a href="http://www.tofteproject.info/" target="_blank">The Tofte Project</a> is a beautifully designed webpage which details the planning, construction and design philosophy of a truly eco-friendly house. The house is really quite amazing! The guy who designed the sound for the Tofte page has <a href="http://www.pilotvibe.com/dec.2002/warn.html" target="_blank">his own page</a>. I really like the idea of the page's user selectable audio themes.

The idea think-tank, <a href="http://www.zpluspartners.com/about.html" target="_blank">Z+ Partners</a>, has <a href="http://www.zpluspartners.com/zblog/" target="_blank">a great blog</a> detailing the future of design, branding, innovation, sustainability and other emerging issues. It isn't updated too often but the information provided is top notch.

I find that I am becoming more and more interested in philosophy. I just finished the wonderfully complex and compelling, <a href="http://members.lycos.co.uk/sophiesworld/" target="_blank">Sophie's World</a>, which, (by it's own admission), raises more questions that answers. I found a little page today with links to various <a href="http://www.angelfire.com/ego/philosophyradio/">radio programs</a> and <a href="http://www.angelfire.com/ego/philosophyradio/lectures.html">lectures</a> dealing with philosophy. I love knowing that I know nothing.
