One of my favourite albums of all time is <i>Daisies of the Galaxy</i> by the <a href="http://www.allmusic.com/cg/amg.dll?p=amg&amp;opt1=1&amp;sql=eels" target="_blank">Eels</a>. Check it out if you have the chance. Or else just check out the <a href="http://www.eelstheband.com/preview/register.php" target="_blank">preview of the new Eels album</a>.

Now that we're on the subject of favourites I'll mention one of my favourite movies, <a href="http://www.prism.gatech.edu/~gte484v/wakinglife.html" target="_blank">Waking Life</a>. A lot of people wrote off this movie as preachy and / or pretentious but it contains some really thought provoking ideas, (not to mention some amazing visuals). Sit down and watch this movie with an open mind. Let the ideas wash over you as if you are the dreamer and the movie is your very own lucid dream.
