<a href="http://www.perceptionalism.com" target="_blank">The Chef</a> and I gave ourselves 1 hours on Saturday night to create a musical sketch composed solely of loops from my personal sample library. One hour later, the composition work was done. After another hour of tweaking, we were satisfied with the results: <a href="http://www.stungeye.com/sounds/pong/wally_glutton_and_chef_quix-4.mp3">4 - A Collaborative Musical Sketch</a>.

Although the Chef had no prior experience with music composition or production, his time-real feedback and added creativity were invaluable; at times it was like a musical debate!
