[Stars](http://www.arts-crafts.ca/stars/) kicked off their latest North American tour last night, here in Winnipeg.

They performed to a sold out crowd at the newly renovated Ramada conference centre. Their set heavily favoured [their latest album](http://pitchforkmedia.com/record-reviews/s/stars/set-yourself-on-fire.shtml), and was well received by the all ages crowd.

Some adjectives for description: honest, theatrical, emotional, humorous, raw, rock'n.

Stars are from Montreal. Their music is unapologetically poppy, and proves that pop isn't always a synonym for crap.

Related: At least [today's pop music is an easy target for satire](http://www.thelonelyisland.com/bingbong.html).
