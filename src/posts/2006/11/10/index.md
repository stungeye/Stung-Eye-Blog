I was in w[Reston, Virginia](Reston, Virginia) from the 2nd until the 6th attending a [programming conference](http://www.nofluffjuststuff.com/). I spent Monday exploring Washington D.C. 

Fall colours hung and fell from the trees. I'll post some pictures soon.

Travelling alone gave me time to read, ponder.

Here's a poem I wrote one night, drawing from the 2004-2005 memory banks. (Mmmmmm, ambiguous.)

&gt;I caught, one morning, a glimpse

&gt;&nbsp;of an ought-to-be

&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;hanging from your lips
&gt;     
&gt;I fought, one morning, an ellipse

&gt;&nbsp;of what I ought to see

&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;shadowed by your hips
&gt;     
&gt;I thought, one morning, we'd come to grips

&gt;&nbsp;with that we sought to flee:

&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;the true purpose of these trips
