<p>My sister Colleen wrote a book. <a href="http://stuffdutchpeoplelikebook.com/">Her book, Stuff Dutch People Like</a>, is an exploration of the quirky culture of her adopted homeland the Netherlands. It’s based on <a href="http://stuffdutchpeoplelike.com/">her blog of the same name</a>. The book is already selling very well in the Netherlands. I am incredibly proud of my little sister!</p>

<p>Although she lives in Amsterdam (and has for the past decade) she’s having <a href="http://www.mcnallyrobinson.com/event-12798/Colleen-Geske----Book-Launch#.Usol2WRDtY6">a book launch</a> here in Winnipeg this Wednesday, January 8th at 7 p.m. at McNally Robinson (Grant Park Mall). <strong>You should come</strong>, especially if you are Dutch, are of Dutch ancestry, or are married to a Dutch expat. I can’t promise you any <a href="http://stuffdutchpeoplelike.com/2013/10/04/no-48-stroopwafels/">stroopwafles</a>, <a href="http://stuffdutchpeoplelike.com/2010/11/18/licorice/">liquorice</a>, or <a href="http://stuffdutchpeoplelike.com/2011/03/06/hagelslag/">hagelslag</a>, but I assure you it will be <a href="http://stuffdutchpeoplelike.com/2011/09/23/gezelligheid-gezellig/">gezellig</a>. <em>I may even wear my <a href="http://stuffdutchpeoplelike.com/2011/09/27/red-pants/">red pants</a>.</em></p>
