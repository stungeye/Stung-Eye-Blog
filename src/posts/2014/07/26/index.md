[Richard Pietro's Open Government Tour - Winnipeg](https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/the-open-government-tour-winnipeg-tickets-12153636855)

<p>August 11th is the Winnipeg stop of <a href="https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/the-open-government-tour-winnipeg-tickets-12153636855">Richard Pietro&#8217;s Open Government Tour</a>. Richard is driving his motorcycle across the country promoting democratic engagement by way of technology, transparency and accountability. I&#8217;m the Winnipeg &#8220;city champion&#8221; for the tour and am organizing the event with the help of my fellow <a href="http://opendemocracymanitoba.ca">Open Democracy Manitoba</a> members.</p>

<p>The intent of his tour is to &#8220;introduce Open-Source, Open-Data and Open-Government to Canadians, hopefully acting as a spark that will help push the movements over the tipping point and change the perspective that this kind of stuff isn’t only relegated to the uber-civically engaged, politico, or super-techie.&#8221; <em>[<a href="http://o.canada.com/technology/internet/its-time-to-make-civic-engagement-sexy-again">source</a>]</em></p>

<p><strong>Join us on August 11 at the Millennium Library as engaged citizens.</strong> Together we&#8217;ll explore the benefits a culture of openness can bring to government.</p>

<p><a href="https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/the-open-government-tour-winnipeg-tickets-12153636855">Register to attend!</a></p>
