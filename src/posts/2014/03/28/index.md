<p>THEY had just met.</p>

SHE, wearing flower-print dress, necklace; purse in hand.</p>

<p>"You remind me", says HE, "of you".<br>
“So I am told”, says SHE, “by you”.</p>

<p>THEY begin to walk.<br>

THEY walk without speaking for some time. Hand in hand in mind.</p>
