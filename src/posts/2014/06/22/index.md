[Understanding Computation - The Lambda Calculus Explained](http://palmstroem.blogspot.ca/2012/05/lambda-calculus-for-absolute-dummies.html)

<p>Historically it&#8217;s been difficult to explain a mechanical universe, because it&#8217;s difficult to explain the possibilities and limitations of machines*.</p>

<p>It&#8217;s now posited that machines compute. That <em>computation</em> describes what machines can and cannot do.</p>

<p><a href="http://palmstroem.blogspot.ca/2012/05/lambda-calculus-for-absolute-dummies.html">The Lambda Calculus</a> is a tiny system for describing computational machines.</p>

<p>*Machines here refers to mechanical entities like molecules and DNA, not dump trucks. ;)</p>
